About me

I am a final year PhD candidate at the State Key Lab of CAD&CG at Zhejiang University (ZJU), advised by Professor Rui Wang (also the founder of RaysEngine Ltd). I obtained my bachelor's degree from University of Electronic Science and Technology of China (UESTC).

My reserach interest lies on the intersection of Computer Graphics and Artificial Intelligence, especially neural rendering, light transport simulation, 3D scene synthesis. My previous work has focused on scene modeling and rendering through data-driven methods.

I'm looking for full-time opportunities. Feel free to contact me if you are interested!
